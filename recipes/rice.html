Rice



Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Stovetop and Rice Cooker Instructions

  1. Measure rice and put into appropriate container
  2. wash the rice until water runs clear/mostly clear
  3. grab pot
  4. boil water in pot (salt if desired, or salt individual serving portions.
  5. stir rice into the water, let water turn to a simmer, stir, add lid, and turn the heat low
  6. check on rice in about 18 minutes. if not done, re-place lid and come back in a few minutes to check again.
  7. wonder why you didn't just use a rice cooker in the first place.
  8. When done, turn off heat, move pot from burner, and let sit for a few more minutes.
  9. take off lid, fluff rice, and let sit for a minute or two, then enjoy.

if using rice cooker (preferred):

  1. measure according to the instructions
  2. turn it on and wait
  3. when it beeps it is done.
  4. add salt, and whatever else.
