Baked Fries

Ingredients:

  • Garlic Powder
  • (optional)Creole Seasoning
  • (pre-grounded or not) Pepper
  • (Sea or otherwise) salt
  • cooking oil (olive/canola/etc)

Steps

  1. turn on oven to 400-450f
  2. Wash potatoes
  3. cut into whatever portions you want
  4. throw into a bowl for mixing
  5. add oil, and stir potatoes
  6. add seasonings, then stir again
  7. once oven is pre-heated, put potatoes into baking medium, spray non-stick oil first optionally.
  8. check on food in 15 minutes to stir and make sure it is not sticking to the baking pan
  9. check on in another 15 minutes, if not done add anoter 10 minutes
  10. when done, serve
