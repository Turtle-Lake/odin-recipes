Baked Chicken



Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Marinade
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. take chicken out of packaging and put into bowl/ziplock bag
  2. put marinade and all seasonings into bag, preheat oven to 400-450f
  3. after about 20-30 minutes, and once oven is pre-heated, put chicken onto baking pan
  4. set timer for 30-35 minutes
  5. check chicken with digital thermometer. If 160-165 (160f has pasteurization time of 15 seconds), it is done.
  6. remove from oven and serve
